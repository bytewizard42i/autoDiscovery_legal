# Video Script Outline: AutoDiscovery Primary Workflow

**[Opening Scene]**  
“Welcome to AutoDiscovery! In this two-minute video, we’ll walk you through discovering your network in just a few steps.”

**[Step-by-Step: Screen Recording/Voiceover]**

1. “Login with your credentials.”
2. “Navigate to the ‘Discover’ tab.”
3. “Select your target network or device group.”
4. “Configure any scan preferences.”
5. “Click ‘Start Discovery’—the scan begins immediately.”
6. “When finished, review your results in ‘Reports’ and learn more about each asset.”
7. “Admins can also schedule automated discoveries.”
