## Major US Pre-Seed & Seed VCs Backing AI and Legal Tech
3. **Y Combinator** – Active in backing AI for legal, compliance, and business automation; renowned pre-seed/seed investor.
4. **Initialized Capital** – Early-stage, AI/SaaS/enterprise focus; invests $500K–$1M at pre-seed.
5. **Precursor Ventures** – Backs AI, legal, and SaaS founders very early (often pre-launch).
6. **MaC Venture Capital** – Invests in hard tech and legal/AI applications at pre-seed/seed.
7. **Afore Capital** – Targets technical founders automating legal workflows with $500K pre-seed checks.
8. **Village Global** – Early investor in AI disrupting legal and enterprise markets.
