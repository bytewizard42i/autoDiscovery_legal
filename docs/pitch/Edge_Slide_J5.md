## 📊 Recommended Pitch Deck Structure

- Product name, tagline, your unique credibility in legal/health tech

### Slide 2 — **Problem Statement**
- What pain point does AutoDiscovery solve? Frame it for legal, court, and health law audiences.

### Slide 3 — **Solution**
- How AutoDiscovery addresses the problem — keep it visual and concise.

### Slide 4 — **Market Opportunity**
- **Key insight**: Every state has a Bar Association → built-in distribution channel

This is your **differentiator slide**. Structure it as concentric rings:

```

Ring 2 (Adjacent): UT, MT, NV, WY, WA — Via law firm footprints
Ring 3 (National): All 50 states — Via Bar Associations + St. Luke's network
```

## 💡 Key Strategic Recommendations

1. **Lead with the "Bar Association" angle** — It's your most scalable channel. If you crack the model in Idaho, the playbook replicates to 50 states with minimal adaptation.

2. **Use St. Luke's as your health sector anchor** — A recognized health system validates you for other hospital networks nationwide.

3. **Multi-state law firms are your force multiplier** — One relationship at Parsons Behle gives you entry into 5 states simultaneously.

4. **Frame Idaho as a "proof state," not a limitation** — Investors love a beachhead strategy with a clear expansion path.

5. **Quantify the network** — Count the attorneys, judges, and health professionals reachable through each relationship tier.
